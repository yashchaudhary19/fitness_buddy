How It Works

Step-by-Step Experience

Take a full tour of the NutriVault workflow and see how it helps you hit your goals.

1

Begin your journey

Start with our premium onboarding experience designed to align your mind and body. Set up your basic details in seconds to kickstart tracking.

2

Select your primary target

Define your objective. Choose whether you want to lose body fat, maintain current weight with healthy lifestyle choices, or gain lean muscle mass.

3

Calculate your daily budget

NutriVault builds a customized daily calorie budget and target macronutrients (Carbs, Protein, Fats, and Fiber) tailored to your physiology and goal.

4

Check your dashboard

See your daily metrics in real time. A dynamic, glowing calorie ring shows remaining calories and live progress indicators keep your macro splits in check.

5

Track meals in seconds

Log breakfast, lunch, dinner, or snacks instantly. Access quick shortcuts for barcode scanning, AI photo scanning, hands-free voice logging, or smart database searches.

6

Scan food with vision AI

Simply snap a photo of your plate. Our advanced vision AI instantly identifies the ingredients (like Chapati and curry) and estimates weights and portion sizes.

7

Review your daily Nutrition

Browse your itemized daily food journal. Spot patterns in calorie densities, check specific meals logged, and review total calories added automatically.

8

Get personalized AI coaching

Consult your Conversational Coach for nutrition suggestions, review your Daily Nutrition Debriefs, and get direct weight advice based on your logs.

9

Analyze your progress trends

Monitor weight trends over weeks or months with clean graphs, moving average trendlines, and a complete history timeline logging your accomplishments.
